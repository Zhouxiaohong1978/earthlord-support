欢迎使用末日之主

末日之主是一款沉浸式末日主题 iOS 游戏。真实走路即可探索废土、圈地建造、与玩家交易通讯，在荒芜的末日世界中建立属于你的王国。

核心玩法

🗺️ 步行探索

真实步行解锁探索进度，在废墟中搜刮水源、食物、建造材料

🏰 圈地建造

用GPS路径圈定你的领地，建造哨塔、仓库、通讯设备，经营末日王国

📡 通讯系统

通过对讲机、营地广播、卫星频道与玩家联络，位置信息实时共享

💹 资源交易

在交易中心挂单出售或购买物资，构建末日经济生态

💧 生存体征

维持饱食度与水分，及时补给以保持战斗力。濒死状态将限制部分行动

🏴 领地税收

其他玩家进入你的领地搜刮时自动扣税，被动积累资源

Welcome to 末日之主

末日之主 is an immersive apocalypse-themed iOS game. Walk in the real world to explore wastelands, claim territory, build your base, trade with players, and establish your kingdom in a post-apocalyptic world.

Core Features

🗺️ Walking Exploration

Walk in real life to unlock exploration progress — scavenge water, food, and building materials from ruins

🏰 Territory & Building

Trace a GPS path to claim your territory, then build watchtowers, warehouses, and communication devices

📡 Communication

Contact players via walkie-talkie, camp radio, and satellite channels with real-time location sharing

💹 Resource Trading

Post sell orders or buy supplies at the Trading Post to build a post-apocalyptic economy

💧 Survival Vitals

Maintain your satiety and hydration — resupply in time to stay effective. Dying state restricts key actions

🏴 Territory Tax

Passively earn resources when other players scavenge inside your territory — your land works for you
